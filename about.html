ABOUT OUR COMPANY

Our company was founded in Madrid, Spain on 2006 and we are serving our service to South East Asian countries, Middle East countries, Some Developing African countries and Spain. We already have almost 10 years experience in electronic components trading business and focus on widen our resources and great offer to our customers better and faster solution with their needs in the procurement of electronic components and we also plan to build larger facility with humidity controlled anti-static warehouse in our biggest electronic components consuming country near future. That should be our strategic locations worldwide enables to respond to customer needs quickly and efficiently. We currently service our noble customers in Spain, South East Asian countries and Middle East countries.

Our corporate goal is to give our customers the best quality service, customer care and pricing. We have a convenient ordering system and can take orders in your local currency, included but not limited to; EUR, USD, HKD, GBP, CAD, CHF, JPY, RMB, ZAR. Electronic components that are listed in our stock can ship same day if the orders are placed by 3:00pm in our local time.

We are an independent distributor of electronic components and other industrial related products. We work with innovative people who are creating technology and building products for factories worldwide. We help contract manufacturers and OEMs in many industries, including Solar Energy Industry, Aerospace Industry, Shipping Industry, Telecommunication Industry, Medical Devices Industry and Automotive Industry. We help purchasing agents in these and many other sectors find solutions to shortages on components as well as their search for obsolete and allocated products.
